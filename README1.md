# Demand-Driven Inventory Optimizer 👗📦

A Python-based decision support tool designed to optimize inventory replenishment by combining demand forecasting, fashion trends, inventory levels, and customer waiting lists.

The project simulates how retailers can make smarter inventory decisions to reduce stockouts, avoid overstock, and improve customer satisfaction.

---

## 📈 Business Problem

Fashion retailers often struggle to balance product availability and inventory costs.

Popular products frequently sell out while customers wait for restocking, whereas unpopular products remain in warehouses for months, occupying storage space and tying up capital.

Poor inventory decisions may result in:

- Lost sales
- Dissatisfied customers
- Excess inventory
- Higher warehousing costs
- Reduced profitability

---

## 💡 Business Solution

This project evaluates multiple business variables to support inventory planning.

For each product, the algorithm analyses:

- Current inventory
- Weekly sales
- Fashion trend popularity
- Number of customers waiting
- Supplier lead time
- Safety stock

Based on these indicators, it automatically recommends one of the following actions:

🔴 REORDER NOW

🟡 MONITOR

🟢 HOLD INVENTORY

🟣 DISCOUNT CANDIDATE
